Are you faster than a speeding bullet, mine stronger than a locomotive, able to leap tall buildings in a single bound? Is it really annoying! The Speed Cap is here to help.

The Speed Cap is a special helmet that "caps your speed" when worn. When you're moving too fast, the Speed Cap's sail extends, slowing you down to a more manageable pace.

![The Speedcap in action when a high speed player puts it on and slows down.](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/VmCcfx1T/images/d9d2c3f51502905fc4b4696656c2688f14639e40.gif)

The Speed Cap also has options to cap your mining Speed, to cut down on accidental instamining of too many blocks.

The Cap is highly customizable. Shift clicking with the item in hand opens a menu for changing max movement and mining speeds (in Blocks/Second) and assorted variations of its functions. The Cap's functions can also be enabled/disabled on the fly via keybinds.

The Cap can also be colored to your liking by crafting it with colored carpet or using dye on it.
